Welcome to my website! My page dynamically adapts to the time of day to create a personalized experience for you. By using JavaScript, I determine the current hour and adjust the displayed elements accordingly.

During the morning hours (5 am to 12 noon), our page showcases a vibrant image and a custom message that captures the freshness and promise of a new day. The background color is an invigorating orange.

In the afternoon (12 noon to 6 pm), the page transforms to reflect the vibrant spirit of the midday sun. You'll find an image and message that exude energy and enthusiasm. The background color is a refreshing green.

As evening arrives (6 pm to 11 pm), the page takes on a serene and calm mood with a twilight-inspired image and a message that promotes relaxation and reflection. The background color is a soothing purple.

During the night (11 pm to 5 am), the page adapts to the darker hours with a captivating image of the night sky and a message that evokes tranquility and quietude. The background color is a deep blue.

Through JavaScript, this web page offers a dynamic and immersive experience. I hope you enjoy the personalized touch and the carefully chosen background colors that enhance your browsing experience.
